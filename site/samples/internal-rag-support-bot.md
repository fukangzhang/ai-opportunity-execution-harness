# Internal RAG Support Bot MVP

Source: https://example.com/internal-ai-idea

Window: 2-week internal MVP sprint.

Audience: a small operations team that loses time searching policy, onboarding, and troubleshooting documents.

No public contest eligibility exists, but the team has permission to use anonymized internal documents.

Local constraint: Windows, 8GB GPU. Use API/cloud LLM calls and local SQLite/vector index for orchestration.

Submission requires a working demo, 10 real support questions, before/after response comparison, and a short rollout risk note.

Decision note: Candidate opportunity. Useful if the team can provide real documents and a reviewer for answer quality.
