# Design Reference

## Context

Project: AI Opportunity Execution Harness.
Audience: HackerNoon Proof of Usefulness judges, independent AI builders, small technical teams.
Format: 1920x1080 landscape product demo, about 75 seconds.
Language: English, because the competition site and HackerNoon submission flow are primarily English.

## Palette

- Canvas: `#eef4ef`
- Surface: `#f8fbf6`
- Ink: `#16201b`
- Muted ink: `#2e3a34`
- Utility green: `#1e6f54`
- Warning coral: `#e1543f`
- Evidence gold: `#d9a429`
- Link blue: `#315eaf`
- Soft blue: `#dfe8ff`
- Soft green: `#dff0e7`
- Soft coral: `#ffe0d7`

## Typography

- Headline: Georgia, heavy weight, large scale.
- Body: Montserrat, readable and practical, embedded deterministically by HyperFrames.
- Data and labels: Cascadia Mono / Consolas, tabular numbers.

## Motion

- Rhythm: hook, explain, product hold, counterexample, broader use, evidence hold, CTA.
- Transitions: overlapping blur crossfades through incoming scene reveals.
- Image treatment: framed app screenshots with slow zoom.
- Tone: useful, direct, product-minded, no pitch-deck gloss.

## Avoid

- Dark neon AI aesthetic.
- Purple-blue gradient hero treatment.
- Decorative card grids that do not carry evidence.
- Claims without source-backed or repository-backed proof.
